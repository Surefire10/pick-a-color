Pick-a-Color.

How to use:

  • Click the blue button to start color capturing.
  • The color of whatever pixel you choose will be ready to be copied.
  • Change the hue and opacity of your color by clicking the box next to the text field. Or just pick an entirly different color.
  • Fill up your palette with up to 10 colors.
  • Enjoy :)

See a color that you like anywhere on your screen? We can help you grab it, change its opacity, hue and more!
